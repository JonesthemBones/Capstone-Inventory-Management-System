Inventory Management Guide

To track, update, and manage the stock items.


Overview

  • This module lists all store items with their details including stock levels, unit prices, and status.

Search & Filter

  • Use the search bar to quickly find products, or use the filter by status to view items based on stock levels.

Actions

  • The action buttons allow you to view product history, edit details, or delete items.

Add or Update Items

  • To add a new product, click "Add Item" and fill out the form. To update stock, use the "Create Outbound" feature and specify the quantity to dispatch.

Status Indicators

  • Colored badges indicate stock status: Green for In Stock, Yellow for Low Stock, and Red for Out of Stock.

Product Images

  • Click on any product thumbnail to view it in full size. Click the placeholder to upload a product image for easy identification.

⚠️Always record stock movement with the audit trail. Also, ensure all quantity updates are accurate and reviewed for compliance.⚠️
