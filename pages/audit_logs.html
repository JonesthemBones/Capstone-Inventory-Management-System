Audit & Stock Tracking Guide

Track and review all system activities, user actions, and inventory movements for transparency, control, and audit readiness.


Audit Logs Tab

  • View a comprehensive timeline of system events showing who performed what action and when
  • Filter by category, user role, action type, and date range
  • Click "View Details" to see complete information about each event
  • Export filtered logs to CSV for compliance reporting and audits

Stock Movements Tab

  • Track all inventory changes including inbound receipts, outbound dispatches, and adjustments
  • See quantity changes, stock levels after each movement, and who performed the action
  • Filter by movement type, date range, or search by reference ID
  • Export stock movement history for inventory audits and analysis

Understanding Movement Types

  • Inbound: Stock received (purchases, returns from customers)
  • Outbound: Stock dispatched (sales, transfers, returns to suppliers)
  • Adjustment: Manual stock corrections (count adjustments, damaged items)

⚠️ Review logs regularly to ensure all important actions are properly recorded and authorized. ⚠️
