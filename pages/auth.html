Privacy Policy & Data Privacy Act

Republic Act No. 10173 (Data Privacy Act of 2012)

The Data Privacy Act of 2012 aims to protect individual personal information in information and communications systems in the government and the private sector. This includes the collection, use, processing, and disclosure of personal data.

Information We Collect

When you register for an account, we collect the following personal information:

  • Full name (First and Last Name)
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • User role within the organization
  • Password (encrypted and securely stored)

How We Use Your Information

Your personal information is used for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your user account
  • To provide access to the Amacar Hardware Inventory System
  • To communicate important system updates and notifications
  • To ensure system security and prevent unauthorized access
  • To generate audit logs for compliance and security purposes
  • To provide customer support when needed

Data Protection and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data:

  • Encryption of sensitive data including passwords
  • Secure database storage with access controls
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
  • Compliance with industry-standard security practices

Your Rights Under RA 10173

As a data subject, you have the following rights:

  • Right to be Informed : You have the right to be informed about the collection and processing of your personal data
  • Right to Access : You can request access to your personal data that we hold
  • Right to Rectification : You can request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to Erasure : You can request deletion of your personal data under certain circumstances
  • Right to Object : You can object to the processing of your personal data
  • Right to Data Portability : You can request a copy of your data in a structured format

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account remains active or as needed to provide you services. If you wish to delete your account, please contact our support team at +639942823551.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data may only be shared:

  • With authorized personnel within your organization
  • When required by law or legal processes
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of our users

Contact Information

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your rights under RA 10173, please contact us at:

  • Phone: +639942823551
  • Email: support@inventorysystem.com

Help System Guide

This guide provides an overview of the help system and how to use it effectively.


Logging In

  • Enter your email and password.
  • Input your password carefully, noting case sensitivity.
  • Click the "Sign In" button to access the System.

Forgot Password?

  • If you forget your password, click the 'Forgot Password' link
  • Follow the instructions sent to your email to reset it.

Create Account

  • To create a new account, click the 'Sign Up' link on the login page.
  • Fill in the required information, including first and last name, phone number, user role, email, and password.

System Overview

  • Once logged in, you will see the dashboard summarizing current inventory status, audit history, and alerts.

System Overview

  • Once logged in, you will see the dashboard summarizing current inventory status, audit history, and alerts.
  • Navigate throught the sidebar for accessing the pages like Dashboard, Inventory Management, User Management, and Audit Logs.

Receipt Upload Process

  • Use the inventory pages to review and manage stock data before saving changes.

Stock Monitoring

  • By viewing the Inventory Management page, you can track the lists of products real-time as well as their quantity and stock levels.
  • A stock alert pops up as soon as the system detected a low stock level from the list of the products.
  • If you wish to dispatch a product from the inventory, you can use the outbound module in the inventory management page to audit the withdrawal of the products that you wishes to remove.

Help and Support

  • Always access the help icon for quick tips with relevance to the current page you are viewing!
  • If you encounter any issues, you can reach out to the support team via contacting +639942823551.
