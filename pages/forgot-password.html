Help System Guide

This guide provides an overview of the help system and how to use it effectively.


Logging In

  • Enter your email and password.
  • Input your password carefully, noting case sensitivity.
  • Click the "Sign In" button to access the System.

Forgot Password?

  • If you forget your password, click the 'Forgot Password' link
  • Follow the instructions sent to your email to reset it.

Create Account

  • To create a new account, click the 'Sign Up' link on the login page.
  • Fill in the required information, including first and last name, phone number, user role, email, and password.

System Overview

  • Once logged in, you will see the dashboard summarizing current inventory status, audit history, and alerts.

System Overview

  • Once logged in, you will see the dashboard summarizing current inventory status, audit history, and alerts.
  • Navigate throught the sidebar for accessing the pages like Dashboard, Inventory Management, User Management, and Audit Logs.

Receipt Upload Process

  • Use the inventory pages to review and manage stock data before saving changes.

Stock Monitoring

  • By viewing the Inventory Management page, you can track the lists of products real-time as well as their quantity and stock levels.
  • A stock alert pops up as soon as the system detected a low stock level from the list of the products.
  • If you wish to dispatch a product from the inventory, you can use the outbound module in the inventory management page to audit the withdrawal of the products that you wishes to remove.
